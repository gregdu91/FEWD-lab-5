The Lord of the Rings

A young and shy 'Hobbit', Frodo Baggins, inherits a magic ring. Far from being a mere trinket, it is an instrument of absolute power that would allow Sauron, the 'Dark Lord', to rule over 'Middle-earth' and enslave its people. Frodo must reach the 'Crevasse of Destiny' to destroy the ring.
